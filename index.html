About

Online Marketing & Web Design Experts

  • 2012-2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Kimbodo first began as a technology we built to assist our billing and subscription services for our design & development clients.

  • March 2015

    An Agency is Born

    We continued to build our tools and our approach in offering the gamut of web online marketing services. Web Design, Development, Social and SEO became a staple combination of our subscription service offerings.

  • December 2018

    Transition to Full Service

    We realized new strengths and capabilities. Our team's strengths in full stack software development and front end design abilities matured substantially due to the combination of our tools and our approach.

  • July 2019

    Phase Two Expansion

    And here we are now, an online marketing agency with amazing experience and technology to offer. We are focused and committed to serving the growth and delivery of our clients most valued marketing assets.
